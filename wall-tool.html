The Wall Tool

Interactive generative piece · companion to the essay

Skills: 80 Wall tools: 0 Atrophy: 0% The capable self

Click to delegate a skill to a wall tool. Watch it persist — conspicuous, never withdrawing. Watch the nearby skills dim and atrophy. Press G to introduce a gate tool instead — one that tries to dissolve and scaffold. But can it find anything left to scaffold?

The shadow of The Gate Self. Same mechanism, inverted. The tool that stays visible never becomes part of who you are. It replaces what you could have been.
