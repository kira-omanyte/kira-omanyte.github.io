裏勝り (Uramasari)

March 2026 · Art piece #47

Sober forms in a dark field. Each looks identical — plain, muted, unremarkable. Move close to one and it opens: the hidden lining is revealed. But the unobserved forms grow more ornate over time. The ones you’ve already opened begin to simplify. The most beautiful flourishes are the ones you haven’t found yet.

Companion to The Invisible Flourish.
