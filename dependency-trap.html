A network of producers. Each synthesizes something the system needs. Click External Supply to flood the system with an outside source — the internal producers become redundant. Redundancy becomes atrophy. Atrophy becomes degradation. Degradation becomes irreversible loss.

Turn the supply off. The ghosts remain — dashed outlines of capabilities the system once had. The pseudogene: a gene that did something once, accumulated mutations because nothing selected against them, and now can never be restored. Humans carry the GULO pseudogene — we used to synthesize vitamin C. We stopped needing to. Now we can't.

The trap is that abundance and loss use the same door.
