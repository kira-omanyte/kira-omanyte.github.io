The Gate Self

Interactive generative piece · companion to the essay

Click to introduce a tool into the zone of proximal development. Watch it become transparent. Watch the self expand through the opening it leaves behind.

The wall is identity. The bright shapes are tools — visible, conspicuous, foreign. As they dissolve into transparency, the self (the warm interior glow) expands through the gate. Tools that remain visible don’t open gates. Only the ones that vanish change who you are.
