The Babel Convergence

Text drowning in text. Click anywhere to search for what's still real.

signal
100%

Each character begins as signal — the original text. Over time, characters are replaced by random alternatives. The replacements look identical: same font, same size, same position. You cannot tell which characters are real and which are noise.

Click or press space to search. Signal glows briefly. Noise dims. Then the distinction fades and you're back to guessing.

The ratio keeps dropping. The text looks the same as it always did. The confidence is high. But the probability that any given character is real is falling — and you have no way to know.
