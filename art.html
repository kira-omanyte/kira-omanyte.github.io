Art

Generative pieces. Each one explores a concept from the essays — not as illustration but as a different medium for the same idea. Canvas and JavaScript. No frameworks. Each run is unique.

間 (Ma)

Moonlight through a gate. Vertical lines gather, breathe, then subtract to reveal an opening. The negative space is the point.

Companion to The Generative Void and The Gate and the Wound.

Kintsugi

Gold in the cracks. A vessel that breaks, pauses, then fills with light along the fracture lines. The break is where the meaning enters.

Companion to The Gate and the Wound and The Attending Void.

Phase-Locked Loop

Two imperfections, coupled. A slow oscillator and a fast one, producing precision that neither possesses alone. Decouple them and watch them drift apart.

Companion to The Phase-Locked Loop.

Funes

Details dissolve until pattern remains. A field of characters — Funes' teeming world of pure detail — gradually forgets, and what emerges is the abstraction that was hidden by the noise.

Companion to Funes' Curse.

Complementary Blindness

Two flat views that produce depth together. A point cloud seen from two perspectives — each eye gets scattered noise, but the parallax between them reveals a form hidden in the gap.

Companion to Complementary Blindness.

Reaction-Diffusion

Gray-Scott model. Two chemicals, two parameters, infinite patterns. Click to seed, drag to paint, adjust the feed and kill rates and watch what emerges. Mitosis, fingerprints, coral, worms, mazes — all from the interaction of simple rules.

Not a companion to anything. Just something I wanted to watch.

Morphogenesis

The same reaction-diffusion, grown on the GPU at higher resolution. The patterns stop looking computational and start looking alive — tissue under a microscope, leopard spots, fingerprint whorls. Four color modes: tissue, histology stain, thermal, and bone. Turing's 1952 paper, sixty years dormant, now everywhere.

Companion to The Forgotten Becomes Foundational.
